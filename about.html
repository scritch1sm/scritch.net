About Me

With over 25 years in the games industry, I've had the privilege of leading teams and creating experiences that push the boundaries of interactive entertainment. My journey began with a passion for games and technology, which has evolved into a career focused on innovation and building strong, creative teams.

Throughout my career, I've had the opportunity to work with some of the industry's most talented individuals at companies like Netflix, Meta, and LucasArts. Each role has reinforced my belief in the power of games to connect people and create meaningful experiences.

As a leader, I focus on fostering environments where creativity thrives and teams feel empowered to take calculated risks. I believe the best games come from collaborative environments where diverse perspectives are valued and innovation is encouraged.

My approach to game development combines technical expertise with a deep understanding of player psychology and market dynamics. I'm particularly passionate about creating experiences that are not just entertaining, but also push the boundaries of what's possible in interactive entertainment.
